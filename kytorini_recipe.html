Cook Time: 2h40m

Servings: 5

Cals: 561

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 6 tablespoons white sugar, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

Preparations

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  2. Preheat oven to broil.
  3. Pour cream mixture into the top pan of a double boiler. Stir over simmering water until mixture lightly coats the back of a spoon, about 3 minutes. Remove mixture from heat immediately and pour into a shallow heat-proof dish.

Making the custard

  1. Place each individual bowl into oven tray, make sure to leave spaces in between, pour hot water into the tray up to half of the height of the bowls.
  2. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove from oven and cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.

Getting ready to serve

  1. Sprinkle about 1 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar over each custard. Working with one at a time, pass the flame of the torch in a circular motion 1 to 2 inches above the surface of each custard until the sugar bubbles, turns amber, and forms a smooth surface.
  2. Serve and enjoy! You shold consume it within an hour to ensure crispness of the sugar layer.
